FAQ

PG LIFE IN DELHI




What is a PG?

A PG, or paying guest accommodation,
is a type of housing where residents pay rent for a room, and sometimes meals, and laundry services.


What amenities are typically included in a PG?

PGs often provide essential amenities like Wi-Fi,
electricity, laundry, and water. Some PGs also offer meal plans and other services.


What types of rooms are available in PGs?

PGs offer a range of room options, including shared bedrooms (double, triple, or even larger) and private rooms.


Can I live alone in a PG?

Yes, many PGs offer private rooms for individuals who prefer to live alone.


Is PG life suitable for students?

Yes, PGs are a popular and convenient option for students seeking temporary housing while pursuing higher education.
